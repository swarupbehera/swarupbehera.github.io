About Me

Hello! I am a PhD student in the Machine Intelligence Lab in the Engineering Department, the University of Cambridge.

I started doing my PhD under the supervision of Prof. Mark Gales from October 2019, and my PhD is funded by Cambridge Trust (Cambridge International & St John's College Scholarship).

During my PhD, I spent 6 months working as an applied scientist intern at Amazon. Before starting my PhD, I was an undergraduate student in Engineering at the University of Cambridge (2015-19).

Research Interests

  • Large Language Models
  • Summarization and Text Generation
  • Automatic Text Evaluation
  • Question Generation and Answering
  • Natural Language Processing
  • AI for Educational Application (e.g. Grammatical Error Correction)
