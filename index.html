About Me

Hello! I work as a Senior Research Scientist at the Speech-NLP Team in Reliance Jio AICoE.

I specialize in working on different projects related to speech and text data in areas like Cricket (specifically Mumbai Indians), Call-center operations (at Jio), and Bio-medical applications (RFH).

I have a PhD from the Department of CSE at IIT Guwahati. During my research, I focused on analyzing the performance of cricket players. I achieved this by analyzing the live ball-by-ball Text Commentary to identify their strengths and weaknesses (thesis).
